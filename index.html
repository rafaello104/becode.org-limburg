What is BeCode in all of this?

                        

                            BeCode is a non-profit organisation that offers free educational programs for anyone who tries out.
                        

                        

                            How does it work? 
 Well, you apply to join our new program and we send you the instructions of what you need to do to qualify.
                            You don't need any pre-existing knowledge to be able to join this education, all we ask from you is a good attitude and a whole bunch of motivation!
                            We supply the laptops and offer help, so you're not alone!
                            


                            Interested? Come to one of our infosessions to learn more! Super interested? Sign up right now!
